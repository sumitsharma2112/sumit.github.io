Resellers SMS 

If you are a Media company, Advertising agency, or Web Developer you can take advantage of Adxenia BULK SMS Reseller Packages and set up an extra stream of income for yourself. Moreover, you can offer all the Adxenia SMS PLANS features under your own branding which your customers will definitely Love

SMS Reseller Solution

The Reseller Solution enables Adxenia clients to earn additional revenue by buying and then reselling online bulk SMS to their customers. A reseller buys SMS credits in bulk and sells them in smaller batches to their customers.

Adxenia has designed the Reseller Solution to provide our clients with an opportunity to resell SMS credits to their customers for individual and business purposes.

The reseller buys SMS credits from adxenia. For example, 100,000 credits cost 12p each. The reseller then sells the SMS credits in batches of 200 credits at a cost of 14 to 16p each. The profit therefore is: 16p - 12p = 4p per SMS credit. Adxenia cannot match this price as 200 credits bought through Plans will be sold at 16p per credit.

The reseller will ideally have many smaller clients rather than a few large clients, and the financial incentive is structured to this end.

The resellers risk is that credits need to be bought upfront from adxenia, and the reseller needs to collect payment from his/her clients. Note that the SMS credits do not expire.

The Reseller SMS Advantage 

  • The reseller can extend their existing commercial relationships with clients to include the ability to send out bulk messages.

  • adxenia does not have travelling sales people or training staff. Many resellers may already visit their customers and be in a position to help the customer register and/or download software and/or provide basic training on using BulkSMSPlans products at the customer’s premises.

  • The reseller could undercut adxenia’s prices.As in the example noted in section above, the reseller could sell a batch of 200 credits for €0.036 each rather than at adxenia's €0.0375 each.
