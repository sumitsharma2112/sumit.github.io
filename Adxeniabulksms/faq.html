There are Three types of Bulk SMS gateways:
  • Transactional SMS
  • Promotional SMS
  • OTP (High Priority)
Difference between Promotional SMS, Transactional SMS & OTP SMS
  • Promotional SMS: These types of SMS are used to send discounts, offers, or promotions to thousands of new and existing customers.
Features of Promotional SMS:
  1. Promotional SMS are sent to the customers to promote their business brand such as discounts, offers, etc.
  2. Promotional SMS are delivered only on Non-DND numbers.
  3. These SMS services are applicable from 9 am to 9 pm.
  4. These SMS will be delivered with a random six-digit sender ID.
  5. API will be integrated with the client websites.
    • Transactional SMS: Transactional SMS is used to send informational messages or booking/order alerts to your registered customers.
Features of transactional SMS:
  1. Transactional SMS is used to send information to registered customers such as OTP, alerts, etc.
  2. Transactional SMS are delivered to both DND and Non-DND numbers.
  3. Transactional SMS are delivered 24/7 with no time restrictions.
  4. The message will be delivered with a six-character sender ID.
  5. HTTP API will be given.

OTP SMS (High Priority): Transactional SMS is used to send informational messages or booking/order alerts to your registered customers.

Features of OTP SMS:
  1. OTP SMS is used to send OTP Verifications to registered customers such as OTP, alerts, etc.
  2. OTP SMS are delivered to both DND and Non-DND numbers.
  3. OTP SMS are delivered 24/7 with no time restrictions.
  4. The message will be delivered with a six-character sender ID.
  5. 5. HTTP API will be given.
